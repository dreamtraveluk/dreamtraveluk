Bath is a lovely historic town in South England.

Head there for ancient Roman baths, beautiful Georgian houses in honey-coloured Bath stone, and a Jane Austin museum!

A great destination for a relaxed weekend.

Visit the Crescent for truly impressive Georgian architecture.

Visit the Roman baths to learn about the history of Bath and explore the ancient Roman lifestyle.

Sally Lunn's Buns

Based in the oldest house in Bath, serving a variety of cakes and snacks during the day, as well as fine English food in the evening.

Pop by for a cup of tea in the afternoon, and make sure to try the traditional Bath buns!
